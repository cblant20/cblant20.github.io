About

You can’t choose your manager; that’s why you have Lumio!

I'm Cai, the founder and lead coach at Lumio. With a decade of experience working in the technology industry and eight years in formalized leadership roles, I am intimately familiar with the R&D working environment and its distinct challenges. I have a BS in Computer Science and spent several years as a full-stack, UX-focused software engineer before turning my focus to leadership. I've spanned the gamut of leadership positions, holding titles like Head of Innovation and Senior Engineering Manager at high-profile tech companies like Spotify and the Wikimedia Foundation.

I am an INTJ, non-binary, neuro-divergent, queer dog parent. I was raised in the Bible Belt of the US but have since lived all over the US and Europe and have now made my home in British Columbia, Canada.

Values

Lumio provides a unique lens into your professional life from someone who has been there themself. I offer personalized coaching plans based on your communication style, availability, and needs. If you want to be held to a high standard and flourish under a tough-love mentorship style, you’ve come to the right place!

Candid

Radical Candor is one of my key values. I tell it like it is and help you cut through the business-speak to get to the root of the matter. Expect blunt constructive criticism and praise when they're deserved.

Strategic

I am highly analytical and big-picture oriented. After listening to your story, I’ll identify patterns in your experiences and help you find a solution. Together, we'll break down seemingly insurmountable goals into achievable, incremental steps.

Insightful

My deep experience working in technology enables me to offer unique insights into your promotion, workplace challenges, or professional relationships.
